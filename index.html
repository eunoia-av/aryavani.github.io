Psychologist

Meet Ms. Aryavani, a caring psychologist with 3+ years of therapy experience. She has earned a master's degree in psychology with honors and is working towards her Ph.D. Her compassionate, non-judgmental approach creates a safe space for clients to open up and express themselves authentically.

Ms. Aryavani guides clients to navigate emotions and find inner calm. Through talks and seminars, she shares mental health first aid tips, ensuring everyone feels understood.

About

Eunoia Headspace

"Eunoia," stemming from Greek for "beautiful thinking" and balance, reflects our focus on mental well-being. "Headspace" symbolizes a safe space for thoughts and emotions, emphasizing our dedication to nurturing mental wellness.

At Eunoia Headspace, we combine yoga and therapy for overall well-being. Our special method blends physical and mental health for inner peace and complete wellness.
