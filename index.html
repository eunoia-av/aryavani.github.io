Therapist

Ms. Aryavani is a dedicated psychologist with over 2.5 years of practical experience in conducting therapy sessions. She holds a master's degree in psychology with honors and is currently pursuing her Ph.D. in the field. With a compassionate and non-judgmental approach, she adeptly empathizes with her clients, fostering a safe environment where they feel comfortable discussing challenging emotions and expressing themselves authentically.

Her primary goal is to guide her clients through a diverse spectrum of emotions, helping them avoid the trap of overthinking and attain inner calmness. Whether in individual sessions or during lectures and seminars, Ms. Aryavani ensures an engaging and interactive experience for her audience, providing them with essential knowledge about mental health first aid while making them feel acknowledged and understood.

About

Eunoia Headspace

At Eunoia Headspace, we seamlessly integrate the practices of yoga and therapy to provide a comprehensive approach to well-being. Our unique methodology harmonizes physical and mental health, guiding individuals towards inner peace and holistic wellness.

Derived from the Greek word for "beautiful thinking" and balance, "Eunoia" encapsulates our dedication to mental well-being. "Headspace" signifies a sanctuary for thoughts and emotions, underscoring our commitment to fostering mental wellness.

We put an end to your search for tranquility by offering a distinctive, holistic approach to mental and physical well-being. Through our programs, we empower individuals to cultivate a balanced mind and body, unlocking the keys to a fulfilling and serene life. Embark on this transformative journey with us and discover a new dimension of well-being.
